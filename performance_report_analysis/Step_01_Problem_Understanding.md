# Step 1 — Problem Understanding
## Phase: Problem Understanding
**Guideline:** Understand domain problem and expected outcomes

---

## 1.1 Domain Overview
The automobile industry generates massive volumes of technical documentation — vehicle manuals, engine performance reports, service bulletins, emissions data, safety test reports (NCAP), and OBD diagnostic logs. Automotive engineers, service technicians, quality analysts, and fleet managers regularly need to query this data to make decisions.

Currently, accessing insights from these documents requires manual reading through hundreds of pages, which is:
- **Time-consuming** — engineers spend hours searching through PDFs
- **Error-prone** — critical information is missed or misinterpreted
- **Siloed** — performance data is scattered across different document types
- **Not scalable** — impossible to compare across hundreds of vehicle models

---

## 1.2 Problem Statement
> *"Design and develop an AI-powered system that can intelligently analyze automobile performance reports, technical manuals, and diagnostic data — enabling users to query, compare, and extract insights through natural language conversation."*

---

## 1.3 Expected Outcomes

| Outcome | Description |
|---|---|
| Natural Language Querying | Users ask questions like *"What is the torque output of the 2023 diesel variant at 3000 RPM?"* |
| Cross-Document Comparison | Compare performance specs across multiple vehicle models or year variants |
| Diagnostic Interpretation | Explain OBD fault codes and suggest root causes from service manuals |
| Automated Summarization | Generate concise summaries of lengthy performance test reports |
| Insight Extraction | Surface key metrics — fuel efficiency, horsepower, emission levels, safety ratings |

---

## 1.4 Stakeholders
- Automotive engineers & R&D teams
- Service technicians at dealerships
- Fleet management companies
- Quality assurance & compliance teams
- End customers comparing vehicle purchases
