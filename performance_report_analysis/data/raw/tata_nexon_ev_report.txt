TATA NEXON EV MAX PERFORMANCE REPORT
======================================
Document Type: Electric Vehicle Performance & Technical Report
Vehicle: Tata Nexon EV Max
Model Year: 2023
Battery Pack: Long Range (LR) 40.5 kWh
Report Reference: TATA/EV/NEXON/2023/011

---

SECTION 1: ELECTRIC POWERTRAIN OVERVIEW
-----------------------------------------
The Tata Nexon EV Max features a permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM)
paired with a 40.5 kWh lithium-ion battery pack. It represents Tata Motors'
flagship electric SUV in the sub-compact segment for 2023.

Motor Type: Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (PMSM)
Drive: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD)
Inverter: SiC (Silicon Carbide) MOSFET-based inverter

---

SECTION 2: MOTOR SPECIFICATIONS
---------------------------------
Maximum Power: 143 PS (105 kW) @ 3,000-9,000 RPM
Maximum Torque: 250 Nm @ 0-3,000 RPM (instantaneous from standstill)
Continuous Power: 80 kW
Motor RPM Range: 0 to 12,500 RPM
Motor Efficiency (peak): 96%

Note: Unlike ICE vehicles, EV motors deliver maximum torque from 0 RPM,
providing instant acceleration without the need to rev the engine.

---

SECTION 3: BATTERY PACK SPECIFICATIONS
----------------------------------------
Battery Chemistry: Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide (NMC)
Gross Capacity: 40.5 kWh
Usable Capacity: 37.5 kWh (usable = 92.6% of gross)
Nominal Voltage: 351.6 V
Peak Voltage: 403 V
Battery Weight: 320 kg
Battery Pack Dimensions: 1,980 mm x 1,280 mm x 145 mm (approx)
Cell Format: Prismatic
Battery Cooling: Active liquid cooling system
Operating Temperature: -10°C to 45°C (optimal: 20°C to 35°C)
Thermal Management: Active battery thermal management system (BTMS)

Battery Warranty: 8 years / 1,60,000 km (whichever earlier)
Capacity Retention Guarantee: >70% capacity at end of warranty period

---

SECTION 4: RANGE & EFFICIENCY
--------------------------------
MIDC (Modified Indian Driving Cycle) Range: 453 km (certified)
Real-World Range Estimate: 300-350 km (mixed driving)
Real-World Range (City Only): 370-400 km
Real-World Range (Highway): 280-310 km

Efficiency:
- MIDC: 11.5 kWh / 100 km
- City: 10.8 kWh / 100 km (estimated)
- Highway: 14.2 kWh / 100 km (estimated)
- Combined Real World: 12.0 kWh / 100 km (estimated)

Range Affecting Factors:
- AC usage: Reduces range by 15-20%
- Temperature below 10°C: Reduces range by 20-30%
- Highway speeds above 100 km/h: Reduces range significantly
- Tyre inflation: 5% range reduction per 10 PSI under-inflation

SECTION 6: PERFORMANCE TESTS
------------------------------
Acceleration:
- 0-60 km/h: 3.9 seconds
- 0-100 km/h: 9.0 seconds
- 0-120 km/h: 13.4 seconds

Top Speed: 150 km/h (electronically limited)

Braking (100-0 km/h): 41 metres

Regenerative Braking Modes:
Mode 0 - Coasting: Minimal regen (feels like ICE in neutral)
Mode 1 - Low Regen: Gentle deceleration (~0.05g)
Mode 2 - Medium Regen: Moderate deceleration (~0.1g)
Mode 3 - High Regen: Strong deceleration (~0.15g, one-pedal possible)

Energy Recovery through Regen: Up to 15% of total energy used (city cycle)

---

SECTION 7: DRIVE MODES
------------------------
Eco Mode:
- Power limited to 65 kW
- Throttle response: Reduced
- Regen: Maximum (Mode 3 default)
- Range gain: +12-15% vs City mode

City Mode:
- Power limited to 85 kW
- Throttle response: Normal
- Regen: Medium (Mode 2 default)
- Balanced performance and range

Sport Mode:
- Full 105 kW available
- Throttle response: Sharp
- Regen: Medium (Mode 2 default)
- Maximum performance, range reduced by ~10%

SECTION 8: SERVICE INTERVALS FOR EV
--------------------------------------
Unlike ICE vehicles, Nexon EV requires minimal scheduled maintenance:

Every 7,500 km / 6 months:
- Tyre rotation and inspection
- Brake fluid level check
- Cabin air filter check
- All fluid levels (coolant, brake fluid, washer fluid)
- Visual inspection of high-voltage cables

Every 30,000 km / 2 years:
- Brake fluid replacement
- Cabin air filter replacement
- HVAC filter replacement
- High-voltage system inspection
- Battery contact inspection (by trained technician only)

Every 60,000 km / 4 years:
- Coolant replacement (BTMS circuit)
- Brake inspection
- Suspension check

No Required Replacements (unlike ICE):
- No engine oil changes
- No spark plugs
- No timing belt/chain
- No exhaust system maintenance
- No clutch replacement

Battery Pack Service:
- Annual battery health check recommended
- Battery cell balancing: Automatic (BMS managed)
- No user-serviceable parts in battery pack
