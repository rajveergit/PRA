SECTION 1: VEHICLE OVERVIEW
-----------------------------
The Tata Nexon is a sub-compact SUV manufactured by Tata Motors. Launched in 2017 and
significantly updated in 2020 and 2023, the Nexon competes in the highly contested
sub-4-meter SUV segment in India. The 2023 facelift introduced updated powertrains,
a redesigned exterior, and enhanced safety technology.

Dimensions:
- Length: 3,993 mm
- Width: 1,811 mm
- Height: 1,606 mm
- Wheelbase: 2,498 mm
- Ground Clearance: 209 mm (unladen)
- Kerb Weight (Petrol): 1,275 kg
- Kerb Weight (Diesel): 1,325 kg
- Boot Space: 350 litres

---

SECTION 2: PETROL ENGINE SPECIFICATIONS
-----------------------------------------
Engine Model: Revotron 1.2L Turbocharged Petrol
Engine Type: 3-cylinder, inline, turbocharged, intercooled
Displacement: 1,199 cc
Bore x Stroke: 74.0 mm x 93.0 mm
Compression Ratio: 10.0:1
Fuel System: Multi-Point Fuel Injection (MPFI)
Valvetrain: DOHC, 12 valves

Performance Output:
- Maximum Power: 118.36 PS (87 kW) @ 5,000 RPM
- Maximum Torque: 170 Nm @ 1,750 - 4,000 RPM
- Peak Power RPM: 5,000 RPM
- Torque Band: 1,750 to 4,000 RPM (flat torque curve)

Transmission Options:
- 6-speed Manual Transmission (6MT)
- 7-speed Dual Clutch Automatic Transmission (7DCT)

Fuel Efficiency (ARAI Certified):
- Manual: 17.01 km/l
- Automatic (DCT): 16.80 km/l

Emission Standard: BS6 Phase 2 (Real Driving Emissions compliant)
CO2 Emissions: 114 g/km (Manual), 116 g/km (Automatic)

---

SECTION 3: DIESEL ENGINE SPECIFICATIONS
-----------------------------------------
Engine Model: Revotorq 1.5L Turbocharged Diesel
Engine Type: 4-cylinder, inline, turbocharged, common rail direct injection
Displacement: 1,497 cc
Bore x Stroke: 76.0 mm x 82.5 mm
Compression Ratio: 16.5:1
Fuel System: Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDI)
Turbocharger: Variable Geometry Turbocharger (VGT)

Performance Output:
- Maximum Power: 113.39 PS (83.4 kW) @ 3,750 RPM
- Maximum Torque: 260 Nm @ 1,500 - 2,750 RPM
- Peak Power RPM: 3,750 RPM
- Torque Band: 1,500 to 2,750 RPM

Transmission Options:
- 6-speed Manual Transmission (6MT)
- 6-speed Automatic Transmission (6AT) with paddle shifters

Fuel Efficiency (ARAI Certified):
- Manual: 24.07 km/l
- Automatic: 19.01 km/l

Emission Standard: BS6 Phase 2
CO2 Emissions: 107 g/km (Manual)

Air Filter: Replace every 20,000 km
Fuel Filter (Diesel): Replace every 40,000 km
Spark Plugs (Petrol): Replace every 30,000 km
Timing Belt/Chain: Chain-driven (no scheduled replacement)
Coolant Flush: Every 2 years or 40,000 km
Brake Fluid: Replace every 2 years
Clutch Fluid: Replace every 2 years
Tyre Rotation: Every 10,000 km
