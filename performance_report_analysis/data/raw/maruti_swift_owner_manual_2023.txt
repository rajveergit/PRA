CHAPTER 2: ENGINE SPECIFICATIONS
----------------------------------

2.1 Petrol Engine (K12N DualJet)
Engine Type: 4-cylinder, inline, naturally aspirated
Displacement: 1,197 cc
Bore x Stroke: 73.0 mm x 71.5 mm
Compression Ratio: 12.0:1
Valvetrain: DOHC, 16 valves, Variable Valve Timing (VVT)
Fuel System: Dual Injector per cylinder (DualJet technology)

Power Output:
- Maximum Power: 89.74 PS (66 kW) @ 6,000 RPM
- Maximum Torque: 113 Nm @ 4,400 RPM

Fuel Efficiency (ARAI Certified):
- Manual (5MT): 23.20 km/l
- Automatic (AMT): 23.76 km/l

Emission Standard: BS6 Phase 2
CO2 Emissions: 103 g/km (Manual)

2.2 CNG Variant (K12N DualJet CNG)
Engine Type: Same as petrol base with bi-fuel CNG kit
Maximum Power (CNG mode): 77.50 PS @ 6,000 RPM
Maximum Torque (CNG mode): 98.5 Nm @ 4,400 RPM
Fuel Efficiency (CNG): 30.90 km/kg (ARAI certified)
CNG Tank Capacity: 60 litres (water capacity)

CHAPTER 9: TROUBLESHOOTING
----------------------------
Problem: Engine does not start
Possible Causes:
1. Dead battery - check battery charge level
2. Empty fuel tank - refuel
3. Faulty immobiliser - ensure correct key is used
4. Flooded engine - wait 10 minutes, then crank without pressing accelerator

Problem: Excessive fuel consumption
Possible Causes:
1. Tyre pressure too low - inflate to recommended pressure
2. Air filter clogged - replace air filter
3. Spark plugs worn - replace spark plugs
4. Driving habit - avoid aggressive acceleration
5. AC usage - reduces efficiency by 15-20%

Problem: Vibration at idle
Possible Causes:
1. Engine mounts worn - inspect and replace
2. Spark plug misfire - check spark plugs
3. Idle speed incorrect - service required
4. Vacuum leak - inspect intake hoses

Problem: Overheating
Possible Causes:
1. Low coolant level - top up with recommended coolant
2. Radiator blocked - clean or replace radiator
3. Thermostat stuck closed - replace thermostat
4. Water pump failure - replace water pump
5. Head gasket failure - requires immediate professional service
